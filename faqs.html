Frequently Asked Questions

What is the New Jersey Energy Portal?

The New Jersey Energy Portal is a resource that provides information and data related to energy usage, resources, and initiatives in New Jersey.

How do I access the data maps?

You can access the data maps by clicking on the "Data Maps" link in the navigation bar. This will take you to a dashboard with interactive maps displaying energy data across New Jersey counties.
